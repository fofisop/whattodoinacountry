When my sister and I were very young, we recieved a card game from our grandmother. It was just images of things you may find on a road trip, and the players would try to spy them. As my sister  and I got older, we added some twists to our game, so it would still be enjoyable as teenagers. I decided to create something simialar to a virtual verson of it online! I struggled a lot creating this, but thankfully my mom knows some js and html, so she was able to help out! I really needed the help, because I was getting so confused with everything, and my mom was trying to get me to write things in camelCase, but I tried to keep the things that she wasn't as influencial on to be random names lol. To use this: First choose if you want to play unlimited mode, or if you want to choose your own goal! I recommend 50 for a challenge, and 15 for a more relaxed game. Once you start the game, click "Complete" to complete the card once you spotted it on your trip! Aka advanced eye spy. It saves some things, but not all. Use your points to buy things in the shop, and grow your road trip empire!
